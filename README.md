# Privacy Policy for Shape Splitter

**Last updated: April 26, 2026**

Baum Studio ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Shape Splitter mobile application.

## Information We Collect

Shape Splitter does not collect any personally identifiable information. All game progress is stored locally on your device.

## Third Party Services

Our app uses the following third party services:

- **Google AdMob** - for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device information and usage data. [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **RevenueCat** - for managing in-app purchases. [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)

## Advertising

We use Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob uses device identifiers to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

## Children's Privacy

Shape Splitter is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
